The Problem

Every investor re-runs the same diligence from scratch

  • I repeat cap-table, IP, and compliance checks across every fund I pitch, wasting weeks each round.
  • The free label confirms I'm eligible, but it tells no investor whether my numbers are actually verified.
  • Valuation calculators spit out un-audited figures that no serious anchor investor will trust.
The Solution

One audited grade every capital channel accepts

  • Get verified cap tables, audited financials, IP ownership, and governance graded once — reused everywhere.
  • Carry a surveilled, renewable trust asset across angels, FCPR funds, crowdfunding, and IPOs.
  • Prove process quality and transparency, never a misleading return forecast.
Product

A trust asset built like a standard, not a guess
