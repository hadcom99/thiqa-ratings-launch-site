About

Building the trust layer for Algerian capital

Thiqa Ratings exists to end the diligence duplication that slows every Algerian funding round. Instead of a one-off, expiring report, we issue a single audited investment-readiness grade that every capital channel can reference.

Our model is deliberately structural: the rating arm is firewalled from any future marketplace, avoiding the issuer-pays conflict of interest that undermined pre-2008 credit agencies.

We grade verified facts — cap tables, audited financials, IP ownership, compliance, and governance — never predicted returns. Grades are surveilled and renewed like an ISO 27001 certification.

Built on the startup.dz label and aligned with COSOB and ANPT frameworks, Thiqa aims to be the neutral infrastructure angels, FCPR funds, crowdfunding platforms, and Growth IPO desks all rely on.
